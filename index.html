About us

We, at WEBCOOKS, train young minds to develop dynamic websites and web apps with intuitive user interface, without compromising its speed and efficiency during the peak hours. At BridgeCamp -, our first up-skilling program, you will learn to work in a professional environment following the agile methodology, developing your technical skills as well as soft skills.
We are a team of committed, energetic professionals with talented web designers and experienced managers available to guide you through the flawless and timely execution of your assignments and project.

AGILE METHODOLOGY

Agile Methodology is a development method in which requirements and solutions evolve in cross-functional teams through collaborative effort.

SMALL BATCH BREWING

We brew FULL STACK DEVELOPERS in a matter of 100 days. To ensure quality, we have limited the batch strength to 10.

Are we different from others? YES.

We are not like other training academies. We follow a different and more effective approach where the whole process is monitored by industry experts, right from curriculum planning to training. We offer a perfect blend of technical and soft skills to the trainees, making them FUTURE READY.
